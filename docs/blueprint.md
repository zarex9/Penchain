# **App Name**: CredenceChain

## Core Features:

- Certificate Minting: Universities and bootcamps can mint educational credentials as NFTs on the blockchain, with student details and course information embedded.
- Credential Verification: Employers and platforms can verify the authenticity of credentials by checking the NFT's validity on the blockchain.
- Resume Integration: Students can link their on-chain credentials to their digital resumes, providing employers with direct access to verified educational history.
- AI-Powered Resume Analysis: Uses an AI tool to parse job descriptions and highlight the credentials required from the student's portfolio.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A confident, trustworthy blue (#2962FF).
- Background color: Light, neutral gray (#F5F7FA), providing a clean and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: A contrasting orange (#FF9100) to highlight key actions and elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' for a modern and neutral feel, appropriate for both headlines and body text.
- Use clean and professional icons representing educational fields, skills, and certificate types.
- Employ a clean and structured layout to present verifiable credential information clearly.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to enhance user interactions during the verification process.
